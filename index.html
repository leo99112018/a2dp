What is A2DP?

A2DP (Advanced Audio Distribution Profile) is the standard protocol Bluetooth uses to stream high-quality stereo sound.

The Problem

Windows forces a basic codec called SBC, which compresses audio heavily, cutting high frequencies and adding a metallic "hiss."

The Solution

This driver replaces the default, unlocking premium modes like LDAC and aptX HD that are usually hidden on PC.
